ABOUT

Kirtankhola Survey & Polytechnic Institute (KSPI) is a premier institution dedicated to providing high-quality technical education and skill development opportunities to students across Bangladesh. Founded with a clear vision to create a new generation of skilled professionals, KSPI stands for excellence, innovation, and integrity in education. At KSPI, we believe that education is not just about textbooks and exams — it is about preparing individuals for real-world challenges. We offer a wide range of diploma and certificate programs specializing in fields like Surveying, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and other technical disciplines. Our curriculum is designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical expertise, ensuring that students graduate with both academic excellence and hands-on skills.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Kirtankhola Survey and Polytechnic Institute aims to enhance professionalism through need-based and time-sensitive education and training. The institute's academic goal is to promote innovation and quality in teaching and learning while equipping students to become responsible and productive members of society.

Our Mission

Mission 1:
Transform lives through education, driven by the power of diversity, inclusion, accessibility, and innovative ways of learning.
Mission 2:
Develop technologically proficient, socially responsible individuals with the highest ethical standards and strong human values.
Mission 3:
Provide a high-quality teaching and learning environment that inspires students toward innovation and technological advancement.
Mission 4:
Foster research and intellectual resources that address industry challenges and deliver sustainable engineering solutions for global challenges.
